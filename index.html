Case 01

Material Traceability

Ensure ethics across vendors

With the help of ConstructESG's technology, you can track the movement of assets alongside recording information and showcasing records of previous assets. Traceability could ensure that any individual could notice the chain of custody and journey of an asset through the supply chain in real-time.
ConstructESG introduces promising value to the domain of supply chain management and traceability is one of the key ways it does this.

Case 02

Carbon Footprint Estimation

Paint a picture of ecological impacts

Carbon footprint (CO2) estimations in the context of construction are an estimate of the total amount of greenhouse gases produced throughout the life-cycle of a building - this includes emissions from energy use (operational carbon) and materials (i.e. embodied carbon, capital carbon).
Calculating and optimizing your building carbon footprint can get complex quickly, but with ConstructESG you can generate these on the fly to help win new clients and meet regulator and certification scheme needs.

Case 03

Governance Processes

Show you're following best practices

Many industries depend on third-party manufacturers or multiple vendors prior to the creation and labelling of final finished goods. The value of ConstructESG in supply chain refers to the transparency in process tracking. As a result, all parties could get a clear view of their value chain thereby ensuring that they could guarantee the efficient movement of third-party products and final project determinations.
ConstructESG's supports the expansion of sustainable and ethical production and the use of any commodity on a global level.

Case 04

Automated Reporting

Do it all in one shot with automation

The continuously increasing demand for sourcing information about products is also a notable factor for emphasizing the need for blockchain traceability. Blockchain could help enterprises in developing a better understanding of supply chain operations alongside ensuring the engagement of consumers with real, immutable, and verifiable data.
In addition, providing this information to relevant authorities can be simplified by using ConstructESG to generate your reports.
